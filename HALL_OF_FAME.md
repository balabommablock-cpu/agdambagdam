## How to get listed

1. Report a qualifying issue via [GitHub's private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/balabommablock-cpu/agdambagdam/security/advisories/new) or `security@boredfolio.com`. See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for what qualifies.
2. Work with us on a coordinated fix.
3. When the advisory publishes, tell us how you'd like to be credited (real name, handle, link, or anonymous).

## What doesn't qualify for listing

- Reports that turn out to be not a vulnerability after triage.
- Reports that violate the safe-harbor terms in `SECURITY.md` (e.g., testing on third-party deployments without authorization).
- Spam / automated scanner output without a demonstrable issue.
- Theoretical issues without a working reproduction.
